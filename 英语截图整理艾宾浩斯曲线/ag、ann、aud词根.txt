
ag词根

agent
=ag (做to do) + - ent (名词后缀，表人)
词义: n.代理人
Our agent in New York deals with all US sales.
我们在纽约的代理商经办在整个美国的销售

agency
=ag (做 to do) + -ency (名词后缀）
词义: n.代理处;机构
You can book at your local travel agency。
你可以在当地的旅行社订票


agenda
-ag (做 to do) + - enda
词义: n.议事日程 (a list of items to be discussed at a meeting）
This is sure to be an item on the agenda next week。
这个一定会议题在下周议事日程上。


agitate
=ag (驱使 to drive)+- it- + -ate
词义 : v. 鼓动 (to argue strongly for sth you want, especially for changes in a law, in social conditions, etc.)
The women who worked in these mills had begun to agitate for better conditions。
在这些工厂里做工的妇女们开始抗议要求改善工作条件

agitation n鼓动
agtator [agitat+or]鼓动者
